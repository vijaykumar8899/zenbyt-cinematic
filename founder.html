ABOUT FOUNDER

Ajith Reddy Vadde, a passionate cinephile from Hyderabad, always believed in the power of storytelling. Growing up, he found solace in films and admired how stories from different walks of life could resonate with people on a profound level. As he pursued his education and career, his love for cinema never waned, and he often found himself engaging with aspiring scriptwriters, listening to their untold stories and unfulfilled dreams. In 2023, Ajith decided to turn his passion into a mission.

With a modest savings of 20,000 INR, he launched "My Pranaya Cinematic World", an online platform dedicated to nurturing and showcasing the talents of emerging scriptwriters. He envisioned a space where these creative minds could freely share their scripts, receive constructive feedback, and connect with filmmakers and producers looking for fresh, original content.Ajith's platform quickly gained traction. The first few months were challenging, with limited resources and the need to build a community from scratch. However, his unwavering commitment and the authenticity of his mission attracted a small but dedicated group of writers and enthusiasts.

With thousands of registered users and numerous success stories, the platform continues to inspire and empower scriptwriters, ensuring their stories find the audience they deserve.
